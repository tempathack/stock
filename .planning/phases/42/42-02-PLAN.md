<objective>
Integrate the StackingEnsemble into the training pipeline as an optional step that runs after
individual model training and evaluation. The ensemble selects the top-5 base models, fits a
Ridge meta-learner on their predictions, and participates in the winner selection process
alongside all other models.

Purpose: ADVML-02 (Ensemble integrated into training_pipeline.py as optional step after ranking).
Output: Pipeline becomes 12 steps. After training + ranking all base models, the ensemble step
builds a stacking model from the top-5, evaluates it, and adds it to the candidate pool.
If the ensemble beats all base models on composite score, it becomes the winner.
The ensemble is persisted to the model registry like any other model.
</objective>
