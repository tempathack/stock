Fix all four candidate root causes:
1. MinIO secrets not copied to flink namespace (blocks checkpoint writes)
2. stock-platform-secrets not in flink namespace (blocks JDBC/Postgres sink)
3. Kafka intraday-data topic unreachable or missing
4. deploy-all.sh secret copy step gap

Output: Running ohlcv-normalizer FlinkDeployment with completed checkpoints. Confirmed secrets, Kafka topic, and MinIO bucket present.
</objective>
