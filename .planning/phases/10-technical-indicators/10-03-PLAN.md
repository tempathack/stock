Add to `ml/features/indicators.py`:

**1. `compute_bollinger(df, period=20, std_dev=2)`:**
- middle = close.rolling(period).mean()
- std = close.rolling(period).std()
- bb_upper = middle + std_dev × std
- bb_lower = middle - std_dev × std
- bb_bandwidth = (bb_upper - bb_lower) / middle

**2. `compute_atr(df, period=14)`:**
- tr = _true_range(df) (reuse from Plan 02)
- atr = tr.ewm(alpha=1/period, min_periods=period, adjust=False).mean()

**3. `compute_rolling_volatility(df, window=21)`:**
- daily_returns = close.pct_change()
- rolling_vol = daily_returns.rolling(window).std() × sqrt(252)
  </action>
