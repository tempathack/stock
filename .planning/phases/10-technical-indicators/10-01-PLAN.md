  <action>
Replace the stub in `ml/features/indicators.py` with the momentum indicator implementations:

**1. `compute_rsi(df, period=14)`:**
- Wilder's smoothing: `ewm(alpha=1/period, min_periods=period, adjust=False)`
- delta = close.diff()
- gain = delta.clip(lower=0), loss = -delta.clip(upper=0)
- RS = avg_gain / avg_loss
- RSI = 100 - (100 / (1 + RS))
- Column name: `rsi_{period}`

**2. `compute_macd(df, fast=12, slow=26, signal=9)`:**
- ema_fast = close.ewm(span=fast, adjust=False).mean()
- ema_slow = close.ewm(span=slow, adjust=False).mean()
- macd_line = ema_fast - ema_slow
- macd_signal = macd_line.ewm(span=signal, adjust=False).mean()
- macd_hist = macd_line - macd_signal

**3. `compute_stochastic(df, k_period=14, d_period=3)`:**
- lowest_low = low.rolling(k_period).min()
- highest_high = high.rolling(k_period).max()
- %K = (close - lowest_low) / (highest_high - lowest_low) × 100
- %D = %K.rolling(d_period).mean()
  </action>
